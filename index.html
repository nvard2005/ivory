Ivory Dentin Graft™ is a novel, xenograft-origin bioresorbable graft material for the repair or augmentation of bone defects in dental procedures.

Ivory Graft Ltd.

has developed Ivory Dentin Graft™ in order to expand the use of dentin-derived material to patients where autogenic tooth material is not available.

Ivory Dentin Graft™ is powered by advantages of unique dentin structure and organic material.

Bioresorbable dentin graft

In its structure, Ivory Dentin Graft™ is chemically the same as human bone. In addition to mineral phase, it contains a full active organic matrix.

Intended Use

Ivory Dentin Graft™ is intended for the repair or augmentation of bone defects in dental procedures. Ivory Dentin Graft is intended for use in a clinical environment and may only be used by dentists or oral surgeons.

Ivory Dentin Graft is CE Marked (MDR 2017/745)
